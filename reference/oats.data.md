# Alpha lattice design of spring oats

Alpha lattice design of spring oats

## Usage

``` r
oats.data
```

## Format

A data.frame with 72 rows and 7 columns

- plot:

  plot number

- rep:

  replicate

- block:

  incomplete block

- gen:

  genotype

- yield:

  dry matter yield

- row:

  row

- col:

  column

## Source

J. A. John & E. R. Williams (1995). Cyclic and computer generated
designs. Chapman and Hall, London. Page 146.

## Details

The response is grain yield in kg per hectare. The design was an alpha
design with 24 varieties, three replicates and six incomplete blocks of
size four per replicate. The 72 plots were arranged in a single linear
array.

## References

Boer, Martin P., Hans-Peter Piepho, and Emlyn R. Williams. "Linear
variance, P-splines and neighbour differences for spatial adjustment in
field trials: how are they related?." JABES 25, no. 4 (2020): 676-698.
